About Us

Welcome to Green Leaf Hotel, your sanctuary nestled near the heart of nature. Located in close proximity to the jungle and national park, our resort offers an ideal escape for those seeking an immersive experience in the natural beauty of Chitwan. At Green Leaf Hotel, we provide more than just accommodation - we offer a gateway to adventure.

Surrounded by the breathtaking scenery of Chitwan, Green Leaf Hotel is your perfect retreat, providing a haven of tranquility and a base for exploring the wonders of nature. Come and experience the beauty, sincerity, and serenity that define our hotel – your ultimate destination for a memorable vacation.

Resturant

The Specialty Restaurant situated on the westside of top floor and The Sky Lounge rooftop bar situated on the northside of top floor, serving specialty menu and relaxing drinks, is like a view tower providing a 360° degree magnificent view of nearby valley.

It is a perfect place to catch up with your travel mate over your favorite cocktails or simply engross in the serene atmosphere..

Reception

Step into our reception area, where a welcoming and cozy atmosphere greets you. Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing seamless service and assistance, ensuring your experience is both pleasant and efficient.

The stylish and contemporary design adds a touch of modern elegance to your arrival, setting the tone for a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Deluxe Room

At Hotel Green Leaf, the Deluxe Room offers a tranquil retreat with its spacious layout, large windows, wooden flooring, and a serene natural environment for a perfect blend of luxury and relaxation.

Things to do

Tharu Program

One of the largest population of the ethnic groups residing in Chitwan is the Tharus. The local performers wearing their ethnic dresses perform and welcome you to know their culture, their place and themselves well by performing the dance prominently using Dhols and Tharu sticks throughout the performance.
